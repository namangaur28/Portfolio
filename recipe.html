shahi paneer

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Onion
  • Fresh cream
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices
  • Salt
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Prepare the base – Sauté onions, ginger-garlic, and cashews in ghee/oil until soft. Cool and blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Cook the gravy – In the same pan, add whole spices, then the onion-cashew paste and cook well. Add tomato puree and powdered spices; cook until oil separates.
  3. Make it rich – Stir in milk/cream and a little sugar for balance. Crush in kasuri methi.
  4. Add paneer – Gently add paneer cubes and simmer for a few minutes so they absorb the flavors (don’t overcook).
  5. Finish & garnish – Sprinkle garam masala, top with cream and fresh coriander, serve hot with naan or rice.
Have a great meal
